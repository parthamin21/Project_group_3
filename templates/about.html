About the Dataset

This dataset contains medical data used to analyze and predict diabetes outcomes. Below is a detailed description of each variable in the dataset.

Pregnancies

Number of times the patient has been pregnant.

Glucose

Plasma glucose concentration (mg/dL) from a 2-hour oral glucose tolerance test.

BloodPressure

Diastolic blood pressure (mm Hg), an important measure of cardiovascular health.

SkinThickness

Thickness of the triceps skinfold (mm), used to estimate body fat percentage.

Insulin

2-hour serum insulin level (µU/mL), indicating insulin resistance or beta-cell function.

BMI

Body Mass Index (kg/m²), a measure of obesity and an important diabetes risk factor.

DiabetesPedigreeFunction

A function that estimates the genetic predisposition for diabetes based on family history.

Age

Age of the patient (years). Older individuals have a higher risk of diabetes.

Outcome

Binary outcome variable: 1 = Diabetes, 0 = No Diabetes.
